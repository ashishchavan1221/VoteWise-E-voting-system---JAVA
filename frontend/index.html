Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Is online voting completely secure?

Absolutely. VoteWise uses military-grade encryption to secure your data and identity. Once your vote is cast, it is anonymized and securely stored in the local database, guaranteeing your privacy while ensuring it's counted accurately.

Can I change my vote after submitting it?

No. To maintain the integrity of the election, all votes are finalized instantly. Please review your choice carefully before confirming.

Who is eligible to vote on VoteWise?

Any registered student with a valid Voter ID provided by the administration can cast a vote. College students primarily use VoteWise to participate remotely in council elections from their hostels or homes.
